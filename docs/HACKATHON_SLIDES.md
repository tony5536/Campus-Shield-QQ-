## Demo Narration Script (30-45 seconds)

**Opening (5 seconds):**
"Welcome to CampusShield AI - a smart campus safety system powered by artificial intelligence."

**Core Demo (25-30 seconds):**
"Here's how it works: When an incident is detected, our AI instantly analyzes the situation. Watch as I describe an unauthorized entry - the AI immediately classifies it as HIGH severity, provides a clear summary, and recommends immediate action. The system uses color-coded badges - red for high priority, orange for medium, green for low - making it instantly clear to security teams what needs attention. This AI-powered analysis happens in real-time, reducing response delays and helping security teams make faster, better decisions."

**Closing (5 seconds):**
"CampusShield AI - transforming campus safety through intelligent automation."
